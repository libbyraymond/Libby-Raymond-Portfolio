Tether

Tether is a digital and physical solution aimed at transforming hiking from a solitary activity into a vibrant, connected experience. During this project I co-designed an app that responded to real user frustrations, like the difficulty of finding hiking partners and communities. My team researched, prototyped, and iterated on features that made it easy for hikers of all levels to identify themselves, join group events, and discover new trails tailored to their interests.

To ensure our design was truly user-led, we began by mapping the journey of novice hikers and expert hikers, capturing pain points in real context. We tested our prototypes with real people from the NU Rock Climbing Club and broader hiking community, gathering direct feedback at every stage. Their input continually shaped our features, from self-identification options to community event matchmaking and trail-finding tools.

I designed a Hiking Style Quiz that furthers Tether’s purpose of an inclusive, barrier-free hiking introduction. The quiz helps users identify their hiking preferences and comfort levels without any pressure or judgment. Instead of ranking users by experience or fitness, our quiz offers a friendly series of questions focused on personal pace, social comfort, and trail interests. The language used celebrates every type of hiker, from those who prefer leisurely walks to seasoned adventurers.

To bring Tether’s community-building to life on the trail, we created physical prototypes and branded merch that make it easy for hikers to identify and connect with their group. Each participant could wear a matching hat or use a water bottle featuring Tether’s colors and branding. When a group hikes together with matching hats or gear, it instantly signals belonging and camaraderie, making it easier to spot teammates, strike up conversations, and share the experience.

These tangible items reinforce the digital experience of the app, creating a shared identity and sense of community that extends beyond the trail. By combining visible, practical merch with the app’s features, Tether turns hiking from a solitary activity into a fun, inclusive, and connected adventure for everyone.
