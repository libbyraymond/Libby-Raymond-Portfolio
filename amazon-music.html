Amazon Music

This project was in partnership with Amazon Music and presented at their San Francisco headquarters. We reimagined Amazon Music by leveraging Amazon's core strength, physical products and logistics, to create deeper user engagement and brand recognition. Rather than competing solely in the digital streaming space, the redesign introduces tangible rewards and social experiences that capitalize on Amazon's unparalleled e-commerce infrastructure.

The solution targets Gen Z users who crave authentic experiences, collectible items, and community connection, transforming passive music consumption into an engaging lifestyle brand that extends beyond the app.

Amazon Playback

Your Year in Music, Delivered

A premium physical poster that visualizes users' annual listening data, similar to Spotify Wrapped but delivered as a high-quality, customizable collectible. Users earn this reward after listening to 10,000+ minutes per year, creating a tangible symbol of their musical journey.

Listening Party

Experience Music Together

Virtual listening parties transform album releases into community events, building fandom while offering exclusive access to merchandise and deals. This feature mimics the excitement of concert merch while creating digital-first community experiences.

My Music Room

Experience Music Together

This feature allows users to browse both their purchased music library and Amazon's marketplace using augmented reality. Users can visualize how albums would look on their actual shelves before purchasing, creating a seamless bridge between digital discovery and physical collection.

Amazon Music Advent

25 Days of Musical Surprises

Capitalizing on the season of giving and the social media obsession with unboxing content, Prime Advent Box delivers 25 days of exclusive artist merchandise at reduced prices. Users customize their experience while preserving the viral anticipation that drives social sharing and organic promotion.

This redesign transforms Amazon Music from a commodity streaming service into a lifestyle brand that:

  • Leverages Amazon's Strengths: Utilizes world-class e-commerce and logistics infrastructure.
  • Creates Collectible Value: Physical rewards and merchandise build long-term user investment.
  • Builds Community: Social features foster fan connections and organic promotion.
  • Drives Engagement: Gamified rewards (10,000+ minutes for posters) increase platform usage.
  • Reduces Acquisition Costs: Organic social sharing and community building provide natural marketing.

By connecting digital music consumption with physical rewards and social experiences, this redesign positions Amazon Music as more than just another streaming platform, it becomes a comprehensive music lifestyle ecosystem that only Amazon could deliver.
